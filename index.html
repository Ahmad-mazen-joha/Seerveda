Strategic Objectives:

Developing the medical products sector: Supplying the medical market (both Syrian and global) with high-quality national products that rival international counterparts in formulation and efficacy, at affordable prices.

Innovating health systems

Providing advanced therapeutic and aesthetic solutions for skin, hair, and wellness care-offering unique, cutting-edge alternatives that meet international standards.

our story

Seerveda Medical Group is a leading, integrated Syrian company in the healthcare sector, aiming to enhance quality of life under the slogan "Live a healthy lifestyle" by combining the magic of nature and chemistry with innovative global technologies.

Keys to Success

Seerveda relies on a comprehensive medical team with deep expertise in the Syrian market, grounding its operations in academic, medical, and marketing research to ensure it meets the aspirations of the healthcare sector and the community.

Meeting market needs

Securing medical products in both the public and private sectors within Syria, in preparation for expansion into global markets.
